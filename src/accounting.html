ACCOUNTING & COMPLIANCE

Accurate, timely financial reporting that keeps your business compliant and positions you for growth.

01 — OVERVIEW

Financial clarity through expert accounting

Every UK limited company is required to prepare and file annual accounts with Companies House and submit a Corporation Tax return to HMRC. Getting these wrong can mean penalties, investigations and missed opportunities. Our accounting and compliance service ensures your obligations are met accurately and on time, every time.

Beyond basic compliance, well-prepared accounts are a powerful management tool. They reveal the true financial health of your business, highlight trends and inefficiencies, and provide the foundation for sound strategic decisions. We prepare detailed management accounts on a monthly or quarterly basis so you always know exactly where your business stands.

At MMK Accountants, we combine deep technical expertise with a proactive, client-focused approach. We do not simply process numbers — we interpret them, explain them and use them to help you achieve your business goals. Whether you are a sole trader, a growing SME or a multi-entity group, our team delivers the insight and assurance you need.

02 — WHAT WE DO
01/ Statutory Accounts

We prepare full statutory accounts in accordance with UK GAAP (FRS 102 and FRS 105) for filing with Companies House and HMRC. Our team ensures your accounts present a true and fair view of your company's financial position, comply with all disclosure requirements, and are filed well within statutory deadlines to avoid late-filing penalties.

  • FRS 102 and FRS 105 compliant annual accounts
  • Companies House filing and confirmation
  • Corporation Tax computation and CT600 submission
  • Director and shareholder reporting packs
02/ Management Reporting

Timely, accurate management accounts give you the financial intelligence to make confident business decisions. We prepare monthly or quarterly management reports tailored to your business, including profit and loss statements, balance sheets, cash flow analysis and KPI dashboards that translate raw data into actionable insight.

  • Monthly and quarterly management accounts
  • Profit and loss, balance sheet and cash flow reporting
  • Budget vs actual variance analysis
  • Custom KPI dashboards and trend reporting
03/ Compliance & Filings

Staying compliant with Companies House, HMRC and other regulatory bodies requires constant vigilance. We manage all your statutory filing obligations, monitor key deadlines and ensure nothing slips through the cracks. From confirmation statements to annual returns, we handle the administrative burden so you can focus on running your business.

  • Companies House annual confirmation statements
  • HMRC Corporation Tax returns and payments
  • Statutory deadline monitoring and reminders
  • Penalty protection and compliance health checks
04/ Financial Analysis

Numbers tell a story — our job is to help you read it. We go beyond standard reporting to provide in-depth financial analysis that identifies opportunities, flags risks and supports strategic planning. Whether you need profitability analysis by product line, working capital optimisation or forecasting models, we deliver clarity from complexity.

  • Profitability and margin analysis by segment
  • Working capital and cash flow optimisation
  • Financial forecasting and scenario modelling
  • Benchmarking against industry standards
