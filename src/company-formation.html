COMPANY FORMATION

Professional limited company incorporation with full compliance setup — ready to trade in 24 hours.

01 — OVERVIEW

Incorporate your company with complete confidence

Forming a limited company in the UK is more than just filing documents with Companies House. The decisions you make during incorporation — from your company name and share structure to your articles of association and registered office address — create the legal foundation upon which your entire business is built. Getting these details right from the start is essential.

Our company formation service goes far beyond basic registration. We advise on the optimal share structure and articles of association for your circumstances, handle all filings with Companies House, register your company with HMRC for Corporation Tax and any other relevant taxes, and set up your accounting software so you are ready to trade from day one. We also provide a prestigious registered office address in London or Luton if you prefer not to use your home address.

At MMK Accountants, we form hundreds of companies every year for entrepreneurs, contractors, property investors and established businesses launching new subsidiaries. Our streamlined process means your company can be live and trading within 24 hours of instruction, with every compliance requirement taken care of from the outset.

02 — WHAT WE DO
01/ Company Registration

We handle the entire Companies House incorporation process on your behalf. This includes checking your preferred company name for availability, preparing the memorandum and articles of association, filing the IN01 incorporation form, and obtaining your certificate of incorporation. We advise on share classes, voting rights and initial shareholdings to ensure your company structure reflects your commercial intentions and protects your interests.

  • Company name availability check and reservation
  • Memorandum and articles of association preparation
  • Companies House electronic filing and same-day incorporation
  • Share structure and voting rights advisory
02/ Registered Office

Every UK limited company must have a registered office address, and this address is publicly visible on the Companies House register. If you would rather not use your home address, we provide a professional registered office service at our London or Luton offices. All statutory mail and Companies House correspondence is received, scanned and forwarded to you digitally, ensuring you never miss an important document.

  • Prestigious London or Luton registered office address
  • Mail receiving, scanning and digital forwarding
  • Service address for directors and shareholders
  • SAIL (Single Alternative Inspection Location) service
03/ Compliance Setup

A newly incorporated company has immediate compliance obligations. We register your company with HMRC for Corporation Tax, set up PAYE if you plan to employ staff or pay yourself a salary, register for VAT if required, and establish the statutory books and registers that every company must maintain. We also set up a compliance calendar to track all upcoming filing deadlines.

  • HMRC Corporation Tax registration (CT41G)
  • PAYE employer registration and setup
  • VAT registration where applicable
  • Statutory books, registers and compliance calendar
04/ Bank & Software Setup

Opening a business bank account has become increasingly complex with enhanced due diligence requirements. We guide you through the process, prepare the necessary documentation and provide introductions to our banking partners to expedite your application. We also set up your cloud accounting software with the correct chart of accounts, tax codes and bank feeds so you can start recording transactions immediately.

  • Business bank account application support and introductions
  • Cloud accounting software setup (Xero, QuickBooks)
  • Chart of accounts tailored to your industry
  • Payment processing and invoicing configuration
