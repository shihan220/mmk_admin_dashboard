BOOKKEEPING SERVICES

Meticulous, cloud-based bookkeeping that gives you real-time control over your financial data.

01 — OVERVIEW

Organised finances, effortless compliance

Accurate bookkeeping is the backbone of every successful business. Without it, you cannot make informed decisions, manage cash flow effectively or meet your regulatory obligations. Our professional bookkeeping service keeps your financial records immaculate, up to date and ready for reporting at any time.

We specialise in cloud-based bookkeeping using industry-leading platforms such as Xero, QuickBooks and FreeAgent. By moving your bookkeeping to the cloud, you gain instant access to your financial data from anywhere, automated bank feeds that reduce manual data entry, and seamless collaboration between you and our team. The result is faster, more accurate bookkeeping with complete transparency.

At MMK Accountants, we tailor our bookkeeping service to the unique needs of your business. Whether you need full outsourced bookkeeping or simply require someone to review and tidy your records on a periodic basis, our experienced team delivers precision and reliability every month.

02 — WHAT WE DO
01/ Cloud Bookkeeping

We set up and maintain your books on leading cloud accounting platforms, ensuring every transaction is accurately recorded and categorised. Automated bank feeds, receipt capture and smart rules reduce manual effort while our team reviews and reconciles your data regularly to maintain accuracy and completeness.

  • Xero, QuickBooks and FreeAgent setup and management
  • Automated bank feed configuration and monitoring
  • Transaction categorisation and coding
  • Real-time financial data accessible from any device
02/ Bank Reconciliation

Regular bank reconciliation is essential for detecting errors, preventing fraud and ensuring your records match your actual bank position. We reconcile all bank accounts, credit cards and payment platforms on a weekly or monthly basis, investigating and resolving any discrepancies promptly so your financial data always tells the true story.

  • Weekly or monthly bank and credit card reconciliation
  • PayPal, Stripe and merchant account reconciliation
  • Discrepancy investigation and resolution
  • Multi-currency account reconciliation
03/ Purchase & Sales Ledgers

Maintaining accurate purchase and sales ledgers is critical for managing supplier relationships, chasing outstanding invoices and understanding your cash flow position. We process all purchase invoices, raise sales invoices on your behalf if required, and maintain aged debtor and creditor reports so you always know who owes you and who you owe.

  • Purchase invoice processing and approval workflows
  • Sales invoice preparation and dispatch
  • Aged debtor and creditor reporting
  • Supplier statement reconciliation
04/ Financial Reporting

Well-maintained books produce meaningful reports. We deliver monthly financial summaries including profit and loss statements, balance sheets and cash flow reports that give you a clear picture of your business performance. These reports form the foundation for management decisions, tax planning and year-end accounts preparation.

  • Monthly profit and loss and balance sheet reports
  • Cash flow summaries and forecasts
  • VAT return preparation from reconciled records
  • Year-end accounts preparation and handover
