RETAIL ACCOUNTING

Specialist accounting and advisory services for independent retailers, high street businesses, e-commerce operators and multi-site retail chains.

01 — OVERVIEW

Accounting that understands the pace of modern retail

Retail businesses operate in a fast-moving, margin-sensitive environment where cash flow management, accurate stock valuation and VAT compliance can make the difference between profit and loss. Whether you run a single shop, an online store, or a chain of outlets, your accounting needs are distinct from other sectors.

Retailers face unique challenges: managing stock across multiple locations or channels, choosing the right VAT retail scheme, reconciling high-volume card and cash transactions, dealing with seasonal fluctuations in trade, and complying with Making Tax Digital requirements. E-commerce businesses add further complexity with marketplace fees, international sales VAT (including the One Stop Shop), and payment processor reconciliations.

At MMK Accountants, we work with retailers ranging from independent boutiques and market traders to multi-branch operations and online sellers. Our team understands retail-specific accounting standards, HMRC's VAT retail schemes, and the operational pressures you face daily. We provide timely, actionable financial information that helps you control costs, manage stock, and grow profitably.

02 — HOW WE HELP
01/ Stock & Inventory Accounting

Accurate stock valuation directly impacts your profitability and tax position. We help retailers implement robust inventory tracking systems, advise on the most appropriate stock valuation method (FIFO, average cost, or retail method), reconcile physical stock counts to accounting records, and identify slow-moving or obsolete stock that should be written down to reduce your tax liability.

  • Stock valuation methodology advice (FIFO, average cost, retail method)
  • Physical stocktake reconciliation and shrinkage analysis
  • Slow-moving and obsolete stock write-down calculations
  • EPOS and inventory management system integration
02/ VAT Retail Schemes

HMRC offers several VAT retail schemes designed for businesses that sell goods at multiple VAT rates directly to the public. Choosing the right scheme — Point of Sale, Apportionment, or Direct Calculation — can simplify your VAT compliance and, in some cases, reduce your VAT bill. We assess which scheme is most advantageous, handle the transition, and ensure your Making Tax Digital (MTD) submissions are accurate and timely.

  • VAT retail scheme selection and implementation
  • Point of Sale, Apportionment and Direct Calculation scheme management
  • Making Tax Digital (MTD) for VAT compliance
  • E-commerce VAT including One Stop Shop (OSS) registration
03/ Multi-Site Reporting

Retailers with multiple locations need clear, comparable financial data for each site to identify which stores are performing well and which need attention. We set up departmental and location-based reporting within your accounting system, produce site-by-site profit and loss statements, and provide consolidated group accounts — giving you the management information to make informed decisions about expansion, closure or investment.

  • Site-by-site profit and loss reporting
  • Departmental and channel-based cost allocation
  • Consolidated group accounts preparation
  • Benchmarking and KPI tracking across locations
04/ Cash Flow Management

Cash flow is the lifeblood of retail. Seasonal peaks and troughs, supplier payment terms, stock purchasing cycles and card payment processing delays all affect when money is actually available. We prepare rolling cash flow forecasts, help you negotiate better supplier terms, advise on stock purchasing timing, and ensure you have the financial visibility to manage working capital effectively throughout the year.

  • Rolling cash flow forecasting and scenario modelling
  • Working capital analysis and optimisation
  • Supplier payment term negotiation support
  • Seasonal trading cash flow planning
