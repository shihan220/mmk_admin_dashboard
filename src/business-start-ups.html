BUSINESS START-UPS

From initial idea to first-year success — expert guidance to launch your business on solid foundations.

01 — OVERVIEW

Launch with confidence and expert support

Starting a business is one of the most exciting — and most daunting — decisions you will ever make. The choices you make in the early days around business structure, registration, tax planning and financial systems can have a lasting impact on your success. Getting it right from day one saves time, money and stress down the line.

Our business start-up service is designed to give entrepreneurs and new business owners the professional support they need at every stage. From deciding whether to operate as a sole trader, partnership or limited company, through to registering with Companies House and HMRC, setting up cloud accounting software and preparing your first set of accounts — we handle the complexity so you can focus on building your business.

At MMK Accountants, we have helped hundreds of new businesses launch successfully across London and Luton. We understand the challenges start-ups face, and we provide practical, jargon-free advice combined with hands-on support to help you navigate the first critical year and beyond.

02 — WHAT WE DO
01/ Business Structure Advice

Choosing the right business structure is one of the most important decisions you will make. We analyse your personal circumstances, income projections, liability exposure and long-term goals to recommend the optimal structure — whether that is a sole trader, traditional partnership, limited liability partnership or limited company. We explain the tax implications, administrative requirements and legal responsibilities of each option in plain English.

  • Sole trader vs limited company comparison and modelling
  • Partnership and LLP structure advisory
  • Tax efficiency analysis for each business structure
  • Personal liability and risk assessment
02/ Registration & Setup

We handle all the paperwork and registrations required to get your business legally established and ready to trade. This includes Companies House incorporation, HMRC registration for Corporation Tax, PAYE, VAT and Self Assessment as appropriate, and setting up your cloud accounting software with the correct chart of accounts, tax rates and opening balances.

  • Companies House incorporation and registration
  • HMRC registrations for all relevant taxes
  • Cloud accounting software setup (Xero, QuickBooks)
  • Business bank account guidance and introductions
03/ Business Planning

A robust business plan is essential for securing funding, attracting investors and keeping your venture on track. We help you build detailed financial forecasts including cash flow projections, profit and loss budgets and break-even analysis. Whether you need a plan for a bank loan application, an investor pitch or your own strategic roadmap, we create professional, credible documents that stand up to scrutiny.

  • Financial forecasting and cash flow projections
  • Break-even and profitability analysis
  • Funding and loan application support
  • Investor-ready business plan preparation
04/ First-Year Support

The first twelve months are critical for any new business. Our dedicated start-up support package provides ongoing guidance throughout your first year, including quarterly review meetings, VAT return preparation, payroll setup if you hire staff, and preparation of your first set of accounts. We act as your outsourced finance department, available whenever you need us.

  • Quarterly financial review meetings
  • First-year accounts and tax return preparation
  • VAT registration threshold monitoring and returns
  • Ongoing telephone and email support
