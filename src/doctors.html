DOCTORS & MEDICAL PROFESSIONALS

Specialist tax and accounting services for GPs, hospital consultants, locum doctors, dentists and other healthcare professionals across the UK.

01 — OVERVIEW

Financial clarity for those who care for others

Medical professionals face some of the most complex tax and pension arrangements of any profession. Multiple income sources, NHS pension scheme intricacies, the annual and lifetime allowance, superannuation certificates, and the interplay between NHS employment and private practice all demand specialist accounting knowledge.

Whether you are a GP partner managing practice accounts and property costs, a hospital consultant with private practice income, a locum doctor navigating expenses across multiple trusts, or a dentist running an NHS and private mixed practice, the financial landscape is uniquely challenging. The consequences of getting NHS pension contributions or superannuation returns wrong can be severe and long-lasting.

MMK Accountants has a dedicated medical professionals team with deep understanding of NHS structures, BMA guidance, and HMRC's specific rules for healthcare workers. We handle everything from annual superannuation certificates and Type 1 GP earnings to complex partnership tax returns and pension annual allowance calculations, letting you concentrate on patient care.

02 — HOW WE HELP
01/ NHS Pension & Superannuation

The NHS Pension Scheme is one of the most valuable benefits available to medical professionals, but its complexity — including the 1995, 2008 and 2015 sections, tapered annual allowance, and McCloud remedy — means errors are common. We prepare annual superannuation certificates (Type 1 and Type 2), calculate pensionable pay accurately, and advise on annual allowance and lifetime allowance implications to help you avoid unexpected tax charges.

  • Annual superannuation certificate preparation (Type 1 & Type 2)
  • Pensionable pay and tiered contribution calculations
  • Annual allowance and tapered annual allowance planning
  • McCloud remedy and pension scheme reconciliation
02/ Partnership Accounts

GP partnerships have unique accounting requirements, from Global Sum and QOF income allocation to seniority payments, premises costs, and practice-specific expenses. We prepare full partnership accounts, individual partner tax computations, and ensure the profit-sharing arrangements in your partnership agreement are correctly reflected in the accounts and tax returns.

  • Full GP partnership accounts preparation
  • Individual partner profit allocation and tax computations
  • Surgery premises costs and notional rent claims
  • Partnership changes, retirements and new partner admissions
03/ Locum & Private Practice

Locum doctors and those with private practice income alongside NHS employment have particular tax considerations. We ensure all allowable expenses are claimed — including travel between temporary workplaces, professional subscriptions (GMC, MDU/MPS, BMA, Royal Colleges), and equipment — and that your self-employment or limited company structure is set up in the most tax-efficient manner.

  • Locum expense claims and travel deduction guidance
  • Private practice income and cost reporting
  • Professional subscription and indemnity fee claims
  • Structure advice: sole trader vs limited company
04/ Tax Returns & Planning

Medical professionals often have multiple income sources — NHS salary, partnership profits, private practice earnings, locum fees, and investment income. We prepare comprehensive Self Assessment tax returns that bring all these together, identify tax planning opportunities, and ensure you meet HMRC deadlines without the stress of managing complex multi-source returns yourself.

  • Multi-source Self Assessment tax return preparation
  • Payments on account calculations and cash flow planning
  • Tax-efficient pension contribution strategies
  • Capital gains and investment income reporting
