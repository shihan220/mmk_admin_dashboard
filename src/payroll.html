PAYROLL SERVICES

Accurate, compliant payroll processing that ensures your employees are paid correctly and on time, every time.

01 — OVERVIEW

Reliable payroll, complete compliance

Payroll is one of the most time-sensitive and regulation-heavy functions in any business. HMRC requires Real Time Information (RTI) submissions with every pay run, auto-enrolment pension duties must be met, and employment law changes can affect calculations at short notice. Getting payroll wrong leads to unhappy employees, HMRC penalties and potential legal exposure.

Our fully managed payroll service takes the entire burden off your hands. We calculate gross to net pay for all employees including PAYE, National Insurance, student loan deductions and statutory payments. We submit your Full Payment Submissions (FPS) and Employer Payment Summaries (EPS) to HMRC in real time, manage your auto-enrolment pension obligations, and provide comprehensive payslips and reports for your records.

At MMK Accountants, we process payroll for businesses ranging from single-director companies to organisations with hundreds of employees across multiple locations. Our dedicated payroll team stays fully up to date with the latest HMRC rates, thresholds and legislative changes, ensuring your payroll is always accurate and compliant.

02 — WHAT WE DO
01/ Payroll Processing

We handle the complete payroll cycle from start to finish. Each pay period, we calculate gross to net pay for every employee, applying the correct tax codes, National Insurance rates, student loan deductions, attachment of earnings orders and any other statutory or voluntary deductions. We produce individual payslips, employer cost summaries and payment schedules, and can set up BACS payments directly to employee bank accounts.

  • Weekly, fortnightly or monthly payroll processing
  • PAYE, NIC and student loan calculations
  • SSP, SMP, SPP and SAP statutory payment processing
  • Payslip production and BACS payment setup
02/ RTI Submissions

Since April 2013, all employers must report payroll information to HMRC in real time. We submit your Full Payment Submission (FPS) on or before each pay date and your Employer Payment Summary (EPS) each month as required. At year-end, we prepare and submit your final FPS, produce P60 certificates for all employees, and process any P11D benefits-in-kind returns. Every submission is filed accurately and within deadline.

  • Full Payment Submission (FPS) filing each pay run
  • Employer Payment Summary (EPS) monthly reporting
  • Year-end P60 certificate production
  • P11D and P11D(b) benefits-in-kind reporting
03/ Auto-Enrolment Pensions

All UK employers have a legal duty to automatically enrol eligible workers into a qualifying workplace pension scheme and make minimum employer contributions. We manage the entire auto-enrolment process for you — from initial assessment and scheme setup through to ongoing eligibility monitoring, worker communications and pension contribution uploads. We ensure full compliance with The Pensions Regulator's requirements and handle re-enrolment duties every three years.

  • Pension scheme setup with NEST, People's Pension and others
  • Eligible worker assessment and automatic enrolment
  • Monthly pension contribution calculation and uploads
  • Opt-out processing and cyclical re-enrolment
04/ CIS Returns

If your business operates in the construction industry, you are likely required to comply with the Construction Industry Scheme (CIS). We verify the tax status of your subcontractors, calculate the correct CIS deductions, produce payment and deduction statements, and file your monthly CIS300 returns with HMRC. We also help subcontractors apply for gross payment status and reclaim CIS deductions suffered through their tax returns.

  • Subcontractor verification with HMRC
  • CIS deduction calculations and payment statements
  • Monthly CIS300 return preparation and filing
  • Gross payment status applications and reviews
