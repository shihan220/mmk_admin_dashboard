CHARITY & NOT-FOR-PROFIT SPECIALISTS

Dedicated accounting, compliance and advisory services for charities, CICs, and not-for-profit organisations across the UK.

01 — OVERVIEW

Accounting expertise for organisations that make a difference

Charities and not-for-profit organisations operate within a unique regulatory framework. From Charity Commission reporting requirements to the complexities of restricted and unrestricted funds, your accounts demand specialist knowledge that goes far beyond standard business accounting.

Whether you are a small community group preparing receipts and payments accounts, a registered charity requiring accruals-based accounts under the Charities SORP (FRS 102), or a large charitable company subject to statutory audit, we tailor our approach to your size, structure and regulatory obligations. We also support Community Interest Companies (CICs), charitable incorporated organisations (CIOs), and social enterprises.

At MMK Accountants, our charity specialists understand the nuances of fund accounting, gift aid reclaims, trustee responsibilities and governance. We work alongside trustees and management teams to ensure your financial reporting is transparent, compliant and supports your mission. Our goal is to help you spend less time on paperwork and more time delivering impact.

02 — HOW WE HELP
01/ Charity Accounts & SORP Compliance

We prepare charity accounts in full compliance with the Charities SORP (FRS 102) and the Charities Act 2011. Whether your charity requires receipts and payments accounts, accruals accounts or a full Trustees' Annual Report, we ensure every submission meets Charity Commission standards and filing deadlines.

  • Preparation of accounts under Charities SORP (FRS 102)
  • Trustees' Annual Report drafting and review
  • Charity Commission Annual Return filing
  • Independent examination and audit support
02/ Gift Aid Reclaims

Gift Aid allows charities to reclaim 25p for every £1 donated by UK taxpayers, yet many organisations miss out due to incomplete records or incorrect claims. We manage the entire Gift Aid process, from setting up compliant declaration forms to submitting claims to HMRC via the Charities Online service, maximising your income from donations.

  • Gift Aid declaration form setup and compliance review
  • HMRC Charities Online claim submissions
  • Gift Aid Small Donations Scheme (GASDS) claims
  • Record-keeping systems and donor database advice
03/ Trustee Advisory Services

Trustees carry significant legal and fiduciary responsibilities. We provide clear, practical guidance to help trustees discharge their duties with confidence — from understanding financial statements to making informed decisions about reserves, investments and risk management.

  • Trustee induction and financial literacy training
  • Reserves policy development and review
  • Investment policy guidance
  • Conflict of interest and related party transaction advice
04/ Compliance & Governance

Staying compliant with charity law, HMRC requirements and sector-specific regulations can be demanding. We help your organisation maintain robust governance structures, manage serious incident reporting, and ensure your charity remains in good standing with all relevant regulatory bodies.

  • Charity Commission compliance health checks
  • Serious incident reporting guidance
  • HMRC charity tax exemption maintenance
  • Governing document review and constitutional advice
