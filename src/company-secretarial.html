COMPANY SECRETARIAL

Comprehensive company secretarial support to keep your corporate governance impeccable and fully compliant.

01 — OVERVIEW

Governance and compliance, handled expertly

Every UK limited company has ongoing statutory obligations under the Companies Act 2006. From maintaining statutory registers and filing confirmation statements to recording director appointments and share transfers, these requirements demand attention to detail and a thorough understanding of company law. Failure to comply can result in penalties, prosecution and even compulsory strike-off.

Our company secretarial service takes the burden of corporate governance off your shoulders. We maintain your statutory books and registers, prepare and file all required documents with Companies House, manage director and shareholder changes, and monitor your compliance calendar to ensure no deadline is ever missed. You receive a complete audit trail of all corporate transactions and filings.

At MMK Accountants, we provide company secretarial services to businesses of all sizes — from single-director owner-managed companies to multi-entity groups with complex shareholding structures. Our team stays current with all changes to company law and filing requirements, so you can be confident your company is always fully compliant.

02 — WHAT WE DO
01/ Statutory Registers

We establish and maintain all statutory registers required under the Companies Act 2006, including the register of members, register of directors, register of directors' residential addresses, register of persons with significant control (PSC), and register of charges. Every entry is kept accurate and up to date, providing a complete corporate record that is available for inspection as required by law.

  • Register of members and share certificates
  • Register of directors and secretaries
  • PSC (Persons with Significant Control) register
  • Register of charges and debentures
02/ Annual Filings

Every company must file a confirmation statement with Companies House at least once every twelve months, confirming that the information held on the public register is accurate and up to date. We prepare and file your confirmation statement, review all registered information for accuracy, and update any details that have changed during the review period. We also manage annual accounts filing deadlines in coordination with our accounts team.

  • Annual confirmation statement preparation and filing
  • Companies House registered information review
  • SIC code review and update
  • Filing deadline management and reminders
03/ Structural Changes

When your company undergoes changes — whether appointing or removing directors, issuing new shares, transferring ownership, changing your registered office or amending your articles of association — each change must be properly documented and filed with Companies House. We prepare all necessary resolutions, board minutes, stock transfer forms and statutory filings, ensuring every change is legally effective and properly recorded.

  • Director and secretary appointments and resignations
  • Share allotments, transfers and buy-backs
  • Registered office and accounting reference date changes
  • Articles of association amendments and special resolutions
04/ Compliance Monitoring

Our proactive compliance monitoring service ensures your company never falls behind on its statutory obligations. We maintain a comprehensive compliance calendar covering all Companies House filing deadlines, HMRC submission dates and internal governance requirements. You receive advance reminders for every upcoming deadline, and our team proactively contacts you to gather the information needed for timely filings.

  • Comprehensive compliance calendar and deadline tracking
  • Advance email and telephone deadline reminders
  • Companies House and HMRC filing status monitoring
  • Annual compliance health check and governance review
