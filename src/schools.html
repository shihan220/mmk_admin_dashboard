SCHOOLS & EDUCATION ACCOUNTING

Specialist accounting, audit and advisory services for academy trusts, independent schools, multi-academy trusts and education providers across England.

01 — OVERVIEW

Financial stewardship for organisations that shape futures

The education sector in England operates within a demanding regulatory and financial framework. Academy trusts must comply with the Academies Accounts Direction, the Academy Trust Handbook (formerly the Academies Financial Handbook), and the requirements of the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA) — all while delivering value for money and maintaining financial sustainability.

Whether you are a single academy trust, a growing multi-academy trust (MAT), an independent school, or a nursery and early years provider, the financial reporting and governance requirements are substantial. From preparing statutory accounts and filing the Academies Accounts Return (AAR) to supporting governors and trustees with budget management and financial oversight, every aspect requires specialist knowledge.

MMK Accountants' education team works with academy trusts and schools across London, the Home Counties and beyond. We provide external audit, accounts preparation, internal scrutiny, and strategic financial advice that helps school leaders focus on educational outcomes while maintaining full compliance with ESFA and charity reporting requirements.

02 — HOW WE HELP
01/ Academy & School Accounts

We prepare statutory accounts for academy trusts in full compliance with the Academies Accounts Direction, the Charities SORP (FRS 102) and Companies Act 2006. This includes the Statement of Financial Activities, balance sheet, cash flow statement, and the detailed disclosures required by the ESFA — including related party transactions, regularity reporting, and fund accounting for General Annual Grant (GAG) and other restricted income.

  • Statutory accounts under Academies Accounts Direction
  • Statement of Financial Activities (SoFA) preparation
  • Fund accounting for GAG, pupil premium and other restricted funds
  • External audit and regularity assurance engagement
02/ ESFA Returns & Compliance

Academy trusts must submit a range of returns to the ESFA throughout the year, including the Academies Accounts Return (AAR), Budget Forecast Return (BFR), School Resource Management Self-Assessment, and Land and Buildings Collection Tool. We manage the preparation and submission of all ESFA returns, ensuring accuracy, consistency with your statutory accounts, and compliance with all filing deadlines.

  • Academies Accounts Return (AAR) preparation and filing
  • Budget Forecast Return (BFR) and 3-year financial plans
  • School Resource Management Self-Assessment tool completion
  • Academy Trust Handbook compliance reviews
03/ Budget Management

Effective budget management is essential for schools operating in an environment of constrained funding. We assist with annual budget setting, multi-year financial planning, in-year budget monitoring, and variance analysis. Our team helps school business managers and finance directors produce the financial reports and forecasts that trustees and governors need to discharge their responsibilities and maintain financial sustainability.

  • Annual budget setting and GAG allocation modelling
  • Multi-year financial planning and scenario analysis
  • Monthly management accounts and variance reporting
  • Staffing cost analysis and workforce planning support
04/ Governors' Reports & Governance

Trustees and governors carry significant fiduciary and regulatory responsibilities. We prepare clear, accessible financial reports for board meetings, support the preparation of the Governance Statement for the annual accounts, advise on related party transaction disclosures, and provide training for new governors on their financial oversight duties under the Academy Trust Handbook.

  • Termly financial reports for governor and trustee meetings
  • Governance Statement preparation and review
  • Related party transaction identification and disclosure
  • Governor and trustee financial training sessions
