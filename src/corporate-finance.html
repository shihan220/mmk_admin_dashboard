CORPORATE FINANCE

Strategic financial advisory for business valuations, acquisitions, fundraising and transformational transactions.

01 — OVERVIEW

Strategic transactions, expertly managed

Whether you are buying a business, selling your company, raising capital or restructuring your group, corporate finance transactions are among the most significant events in the life of any business. The financial, tax and legal complexities involved demand specialist expertise and a disciplined approach to protect your interests and maximise value.

Our corporate finance team provides comprehensive advisory services across the full spectrum of transactions. From initial valuation and strategic planning through to negotiation support, due diligence and completion, we guide business owners and management teams through every stage of the deal process. We work alongside your legal advisers to ensure all financial aspects are thoroughly addressed.

At MMK Accountants, we bring a hands-on, owner-manager-focused approach to corporate finance. We understand that for many business owners, a sale or acquisition is a once-in-a-lifetime event. Our team provides the expert guidance, rigorous analysis and practical support you need to achieve the best possible outcome with confidence.

02 — WHAT WE DO
01/ Business Valuations

Understanding the true value of a business is fundamental to any transaction, dispute resolution or strategic planning exercise. We prepare detailed, independent business valuations using established methodologies including discounted cash flow (DCF), earnings multiples, net asset value and comparable transactions analysis. Our valuations are rigorous, defensible and tailored to the specific purpose — whether for sale negotiations, shareholder disputes, tax planning or financial reporting.

  • Discounted cash flow and earnings multiple valuations
  • Net asset value and break-up value assessments
  • Shareholder dispute and matrimonial valuations
  • HMRC share valuation negotiations (Section 431 elections)
02/ Mergers & Acquisitions

Buying or selling a business requires meticulous planning, thorough analysis and skilled negotiation. We support business owners on both sides of the transaction — helping sellers prepare their business for sale and maximise value, and helping buyers identify targets, structure deals and negotiate favourable terms. Our involvement from the earliest stages ensures the financial aspects of the transaction are robust and aligned with your strategic objectives.

  • Business sale preparation and exit planning
  • Acquisition target identification and evaluation
  • Deal structuring (share purchase vs asset purchase)
  • Negotiation support and heads of terms advisory
03/ Fundraising

Securing the right type of funding at the right time can be transformative for your business. We help you navigate the fundraising landscape — from traditional bank lending and asset finance through to equity investment, venture capital and government-backed schemes such as SEIS and EIS. We prepare investor-ready financial projections, pitch materials and business plans that clearly articulate your opportunity and growth potential.

  • Funding strategy and options analysis
  • SEIS and EIS advance assurance applications
  • Financial projections and investor pitch preparation
  • Bank and investor introductions
04/ Financial Due Diligence

Due diligence is the critical process of verifying the financial information presented by a target business before you commit to a purchase. Our due diligence reports provide a thorough, independent analysis of the target's financial position, quality of earnings, working capital requirements, contingent liabilities and key risks. We identify deal-breakers, highlight areas requiring warranty protection and provide the financial intelligence you need to make a confident investment decision.

  • Quality of earnings and normalised profit analysis
  • Working capital assessment and completion accounts
  • Contingent liability and risk identification
  • Post-acquisition integration financial planning
