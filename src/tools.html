03 — KEY TAX DEADLINES 2025/26
5APR

End of 2024/25 Tax Year

Last day of the current tax year. Ensure all tax planning for 2024/25 is finalised.

6APR

Start of 2025/26 Tax Year

New tax rates and allowances come into effect. Review your tax planning strategy.

7JUL

Second Payment on Account

Second self-assessment payment on account for 2024/25 due to HMRC.

5OCT

Self-Assessment Registration

Deadline to register for self-assessment if newly self-employed or have untaxed income.

31OCT

Paper Tax Return Deadline

Last day to submit paper self-assessment tax returns for 2024/25.

Online Tax Return & Payment

Online self-assessment deadline AND payment of tax due for 2024/25. First payment on account for 2025/26.
