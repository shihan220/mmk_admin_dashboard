CLOUD ACCOUNTING

Access your finances anytime, anywhere with Xero cloud accounting — powered by MMK expertise.

01 — XERO CERTIFIED PARTNER

Your Finances, Anywhere

MMK Accountants is an accredited Xero partner, bringing you the power of cloud accounting combined with our expert advisory services.

Xero is an award-winning online accounting platform designed to make managing your business finances simpler, smarter, and more efficient. Access your accounts from any device, at any time, in a fully secure environment.

xero
Certified AdvisorAccredited Xero Partner

Access Anywhere

Log in from your desktop, laptop, tablet or smartphone. Your financial data is always at your fingertips in a secure cloud environment.

Multi-User Collaboration

Invite your team, bookkeeper, or accountant to access your data with granular permission controls for each user.

Automatic Bank Feeds

Bank transactions, credit card payments, and PayPal are automatically imported and categorised, saving hours of manual data entry.

Multi-Currency

Invoice clients, reconcile accounts, and make payments in multiple currencies with real-time exchange rate updates.

02 — WHY CLOUD ACCOUNTING
01

Real-Time Financial Data

See your cash position, outstanding invoices, and expenses in real-time. Make informed business decisions based on up-to-the-minute data rather than outdated spreadsheets.

02

Automated Invoicing

Create professional invoices in seconds, set up recurring invoices, accept online payments, and automatically chase overdue payments. Get paid faster with less admin.

03

MTD Compatible

Xero is fully Making Tax Digital compliant for VAT and will be ready for MTD for Income Tax. Submit your VAT returns directly to HMRC from within the software.

04

Cash Flow Management

Track money coming in and going out with cash flow projections. Set up scheduled payments, reconcile in real-time, and always know your financial position.

05

750+ App Integrations

Connect Xero with your existing business tools — from Stripe and PayPal to Shopify and HubSpot. Automate workflows across your entire business.

06

Bank-Level Security

Your data is protected with 256-bit SSL encryption, multi-factor authentication, and regular security audits. Xero stores data in multiple secure data centres.

03 — HOW WE HELP

Our Cloud Accounting Services

01

Setup & Migration

We'll set up your Xero account, connect your bank feeds, import your historical data, and migrate from your previous system seamlessly.

02

Training & Support

Comprehensive training for you and your team on how to use Xero effectively. Ongoing support whenever you need help.

03

Bookkeeping & Reconciliation

We can manage your day-to-day bookkeeping within Xero, ensuring your records are accurate and up-to-date at all times.

04

Reporting & Advisory

Generate meaningful financial reports, track KPIs, and receive proactive advisory based on your real-time data.
