TAX PLANNING & ADVISORY

Strategic tax advice that minimises your liability, maximises reliefs and keeps you fully compliant with HMRC.

01 — OVERVIEW

Intelligent tax planning for lasting savings

The UK tax system is one of the most complex in the world, and it changes every year. Without proactive, expert tax advice, individuals and businesses routinely pay more tax than they need to. Effective tax planning is not about evasion or aggressive schemes — it is about understanding the legislation thoroughly and arranging your affairs in the most tax-efficient manner permitted by law.

Our tax advisory team provides comprehensive planning and compliance services covering personal tax, corporation tax, capital gains tax, inheritance tax and VAT. We take a holistic view of your financial position, considering how different taxes interact and identifying opportunities to minimise your overall liability. From salary and dividend extraction strategies for company directors to succession planning and estate structuring, we deliver tailored advice that makes a measurable difference.

At MMK Accountants, we combine technical depth with commercial pragmatism. We stay ahead of legislative changes, HMRC guidance and tribunal decisions so our advice is always current and robust. Whether you are an individual, a growing business or a property investor, our team ensures you take advantage of every relief and allowance available to you.

02 — WHAT WE DO
01/ Personal Tax Planning

We prepare and file Self Assessment tax returns for individuals, sole traders, landlords and partners, ensuring all income is correctly reported and every available relief and allowance is claimed. Beyond compliance, we provide proactive planning advice on salary and dividend extraction, pension contributions, ISA and investment structuring, and the interaction between income tax and National Insurance. For company directors, we model the most tax-efficient remuneration strategy each year based on current rates and thresholds.

  • Self Assessment tax return preparation and filing
  • Salary, dividend and pension extraction planning
  • Rental income and property tax advisory
  • HMRC investigation and enquiry support
02/ Corporation Tax

We prepare and file Corporation Tax returns (CT600) for companies of all sizes, computing the tax liability accurately and identifying all available reliefs. Our proactive planning identifies opportunities to reduce your effective tax rate through Research and Development (R&D) tax credits, capital allowances (including the Annual Investment Allowance and super-deduction where applicable), group relief, and loss utilisation strategies. We also advise on the associated company rules and marginal relief calculations introduced for companies with profits between the small profits rate and main rate thresholds.

  • CT600 Corporation Tax return preparation and filing
  • R&D tax credit claims (SME and RDEC schemes)
  • Capital allowances review and optimisation
  • Group relief and loss utilisation planning
03/ Capital Gains Tax

Disposing of assets — whether business shares, investment properties or personal assets — can trigger significant Capital Gains Tax liabilities. We advise on the timing and structuring of disposals to minimise CGT, making full use of the annual exempt amount, Business Asset Disposal Relief (formerly Entrepreneurs' Relief), Investors' Relief, holdover relief and principal private residence relief. For property disposals, we ensure compliance with the 60-day reporting requirement for UK residential property gains.

  • Asset disposal planning and CGT computation
  • Business Asset Disposal Relief (Entrepreneurs' Relief) claims
  • Property CGT calculations and 60-day reporting
  • Share reorganisation and holdover relief advisory
04/ Inheritance Tax & Estate Planning

Inheritance Tax (IHT) can reduce the value of your estate by up to 40% on amounts above the nil-rate band. Effective estate planning, started early, can significantly reduce or even eliminate your IHT exposure. We advise on the use of exemptions, trusts, lifetime gifts, Business Property Relief and Agricultural Property Relief to protect your wealth for future generations. We also prepare IHT returns (IHT400) for executors and administrators, and liaise with HMRC on your behalf throughout the probate process.

  • Estate planning and IHT mitigation strategies
  • Trust creation and ongoing trust tax compliance
  • Business Property Relief and Agricultural Property Relief
  • IHT400 return preparation and HMRC liaison
05/ VAT

Value Added Tax is complex, with numerous schemes, rates, exemptions and partial exemption rules that can significantly affect your cash flow and bottom line. We advise on VAT registration (including voluntary registration where beneficial), help you select the most advantageous VAT scheme — standard, flat rate or cash accounting — and prepare and file your quarterly VAT returns accurately and on time. For businesses with exempt or partially exempt supplies, we manage the partial exemption calculation to ensure you recover the maximum input VAT permitted.

  • VAT registration and scheme selection advisory
  • Quarterly VAT return preparation and filing
  • Partial exemption calculations and annual adjustments
  • Making Tax Digital (MTD) for VAT compliance
