CAREERS AT MMK

Build your career with a firm that values your growth, expertise and ambition.

01 — LIFE AT MMK

A Firm Where You Matter

At MMK Accountants, we believe that our people are our greatest asset. We are a close-knit team of dedicated professionals who share a passion for delivering outstanding client service and a commitment to continuous improvement.

We foster an environment where every team member is valued, supported and given genuine opportunities to develop their career. Whether you are a recent graduate embarking on your professional journey or an experienced accountant looking for your next challenge, we offer a workplace where your contributions make a real difference.

With offices in the prestigious One Canada Square at Canary Wharf and at Capability Green in Luton, our team members benefit from modern, well-equipped working environments in excellent locations with strong transport links.

  • Collaborative Culture — We work as a team, supporting one another and sharing knowledge to deliver the best outcomes for our clients and each other.
  • Client-Focused — We take pride in building meaningful relationships with our clients, providing proactive advice that genuinely helps their businesses succeed.
  • Innovation-Driven — We embrace modern technology and cloud-based tools, staying ahead of industry changes to provide efficient, forward-thinking services.
  • Integrity First — We uphold the highest professional and ethical standards in everything we do, guided by our ACCA membership and values.
  • Growth Mindset — We invest in our people and actively encourage continuous learning, whether through professional qualifications or on-the-job development.
02 — WHY JOIN US

Benefits of Working at MMK

ACCA Study Support

We fully support staff pursuing their ACCA qualification, including paid study leave, course fee contributions, and mentoring from our qualified team members.

Professional Development

Ongoing CPD training, exposure to a diverse client portfolio across multiple sectors, and a clear career progression pathway from trainee to senior roles.

Flexible Working

We understand the importance of work-life balance. We offer flexible working arrangements, including hybrid working options for eligible roles.

Prime Locations

Work from our offices at One Canada Square in Canary Wharf, London, or at Capability Green in Luton — both with excellent transport connections and modern facilities.

Diverse Client Work

Gain experience across a broad range of clients, from owner-managed businesses and start-ups to high net worth individuals and specialist sectors including property, medical, and education.

Supportive Environment

A friendly, approachable team that genuinely cares about your wellbeing and professional growth. Regular team events and an open-door management culture.
