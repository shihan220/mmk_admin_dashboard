CONSULTANT & CONTRACTOR ACCOUNTING

Tailored accounting solutions for freelance consultants, IT contractors and independent professionals operating through limited companies or sole trader structures.

01 — OVERVIEW

Navigate IR35 with confidence and keep more of what you earn

The UK consulting and contracting landscape has changed significantly since the introduction of the off-payroll working rules (IR35) into the private sector in April 2021. Whether you operate through a Personal Service Company (PSC), an umbrella company, or as a sole trader, understanding your tax position has never been more critical.

Consultants and contractors face unique challenges: managing cash flow between contracts, understanding what expenses are legitimately deductible, structuring salary and dividend withdrawals efficiently, and ensuring compliance with IR35 legislation. Getting any of these wrong can result in unexpected tax bills, penalties, or HMRC investigations.

At MMK Accountants, we specialise in supporting consultants across IT, management, engineering, finance, and creative sectors. We provide proactive IR35 assessments, year-round tax planning, and fully managed accounting services that allow you to focus on delivering for your clients while we handle HMRC, Companies House and everything in between.

02 — HOW WE HELP
01/ IR35 Assessment & Compliance

IR35 determines whether a contractor is genuinely self-employed or effectively an employee for tax purposes. We carry out thorough contract reviews using HMRC's own CEST tool alongside our professional judgement, provide written assessments, and advise on practical steps to demonstrate genuine self-employment. If a determination goes against you, we can help you challenge it.

  • Contract review and IR35 status determination
  • CEST tool analysis with professional commentary
  • Working practices review and evidence gathering
  • Status Determination Statement (SDS) response support
02/ Contractor Accounts & Tax Returns

We manage your limited company accounts from incorporation to year-end, including statutory accounts preparation, Corporation Tax returns, VAT returns, and your personal Self Assessment. Our cloud-based systems give you real-time visibility of your finances, and we handle all filing deadlines with Companies House and HMRC on your behalf.

  • Annual statutory accounts and Corporation Tax returns
  • Personal Self Assessment tax returns
  • VAT registration and quarterly return filing
  • Companies House confirmation statements and filings
03/ Tax-Efficient Structuring

We advise on the most tax-efficient way to extract profits from your limited company, balancing salary, dividends and pension contributions against your personal circumstances. We also consider the impact of the annual investment allowance, research and development tax credits where applicable, and the optimal timing of profit extraction to minimise your overall tax burden.

  • Optimal salary and dividend strategy
  • Pension contribution planning and employer contributions
  • Annual investment allowance and capital allowances
  • Year-end tax planning and profit extraction timing
04/ Expense Management

Many contractors either overclaim expenses and risk HMRC scrutiny, or underclaim and pay more tax than necessary. We provide clear, up-to-date guidance on what you can and cannot claim, set up efficient expense tracking systems, and ensure every legitimate business expense is captured to reduce your Corporation Tax liability.

  • Allowable expense guidance tailored to your sector
  • Home office and travel expense calculations
  • Receipt capture and cloud bookkeeping setup
  • Flat rate mileage and subsistence claims
