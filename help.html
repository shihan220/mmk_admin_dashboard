Frequently asked questions and helpful information about our services.

How Do I Contact MMK Accountants?

You can reach us through several channels:

  • Phone (London): Call us on 020 3981 5373 during office hours (Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM)
  • Phone (Luton): Call us on 01582 726 600 during office hours
  • Email: Send us an email at mail@mmkaccountants.com and we will respond within one working day
  • Contact Form: Complete the contact form on our Contact page and a member of our team will get back to you

For urgent matters outside office hours, please send us an email and we will respond as soon as possible on the next working day.

How Do I Request a Quote?

We offer free, no-obligation quotes for all of our services. To request a quote:

  • Click the "Free Consultation" button in the navigation bar on any page
  • Complete the enquiry form with details of your requirements
  • Alternatively, call us directly or send an email outlining the services you need

When requesting a quote, it is helpful to provide as much detail as possible about your situation, including your business type, turnover, number of employees, and the specific services you are interested in. This allows us to provide a more accurate and tailored quote.

Our fees are fixed and agreed in advance — there are no hidden charges or surprises.

How Do I Book a Consultation?

We offer a free initial consultation to discuss your requirements and how we can help. You can book a consultation by:

  • Calling our London office on 020 3981 5373 or Luton office on 01582 726 600
  • Emailing us at mail@mmkaccountants.com with your preferred dates and times
  • Completing the contact form on our Contact page

Consultations can be held in person at either of our offices, by video call, or by telephone — whichever is most convenient for you. The initial meeting is completely free and there is no obligation to proceed.

How Do I Make a Complaint?

We are committed to providing a high-quality service. If you are dissatisfied with any aspect of our service, we want to know about it so that we can put things right.

To make a complaint:

  • Step 1: Contact your client manager in the first instance. Many issues can be resolved quickly and informally at this stage
  • Step 2: If you are not satisfied with the response, please put your complaint in writing to our Managing Director at mail@mmkaccountants.com or by post to our London office address
  • Step 3: We will acknowledge your written complaint within 5 working days and provide a full response within 20 working days
  • Step 4: If you remain dissatisfied after our internal complaints process, you may refer the matter to our professional body, the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA), who can be contacted at www.accaglobal.com

We take all complaints seriously and use feedback to improve our services. A copy of our full complaints procedure is available on request.
